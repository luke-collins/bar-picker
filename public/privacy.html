Privacy Policy

What we collect

Name. When you create or join a room, the name you type is shown to other players in that room so everyone can see who's playing and whose turn it is. It isn't linked to any account, isn't shared outside the room, and isn't used for tracking or advertising.

Push notification token. If you allow notifications, your device registers a token with Apple Push Notification service (APNs) so we can alert you when it becomes your turn. This token isn't linked to your identity, isn't shared with any third party, and isn't used for tracking or advertising.

What we don't collect

We don't collect location data. Searching for bars by city or zip code just uses the text you type — the app never reads your device's GPS location. We also don't use analytics or advertising services of any kind, and we never share or sell data to third parties.

How long we keep it

Names and push tokens exist only for as long as a game room is active. Once a room ends, that data is not retained or persisted long-term.
